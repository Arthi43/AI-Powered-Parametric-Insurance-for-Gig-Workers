# AI-Powered Parametric Insurance for Gig Workers

## Problem Statement

Delivery partners (Zomato, Swiggy, etc.) lose income due to external disruptions like heavy rain, pollution, or curfews. Currently, they have no financial protection against such events.

---

## Solution

We propose an **AI-powered parametric insurance platform** that automatically compensates gig workers for income loss caused by external disruptions.

* No manual claim process
* Real-time monitoring
* Instant payouts

---

## Target Persona

Food delivery partners working in urban areas.

---

## How It Works

### 1. User Registration

* Simple onboarding with location and platform details.

### 2. Weekly Insurance Plan

* AI calculates dynamic weekly premium based on risk.

### 3. AI Risk Assessment

* Uses weather, pollution, and historical data.

### 4. Parametric Triggers

Automatic triggers based on:

* Heavy Rain
* Extreme Heat
* High Pollution
* Curfew / Zone Closure

### 5. Automatic Claims

* Claims triggered automatically when conditions are met.

### 6. Instant Payout

* Compensation credited via UPI (mock integration).
